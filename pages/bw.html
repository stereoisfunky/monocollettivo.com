White and Black

Video Installation. Bassano del Grappa Italy - 2013

Concept

White is not black, this seems obvious, but in reality things are not as they seem. The point of view of each new subject works in an original way with the object. That's why it is always unique and original. Things changes according to the beholder.

What

The user is encouraged to approach the screen for viewing the images. The face of a girl painting change color as you come closer: from black to white. Things change depending on whether you change the viewing perspective. Video installation in collaboration with Tina.
