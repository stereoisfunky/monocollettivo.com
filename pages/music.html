Mal De Testa Records

former Sound Engineer with a big passion for music, Daniel transformed an old building in a recording studio called "Mal De Testa Records". Since 2006, Daniel has recorded more tha 50 bands of any kind of music, from electronic to death metal.

Syn-

syn- is an electronic music project between Roygbiv and Stereo - founded in Berlin in 2015. Duo experiments with a multitude of beat driven everyday sounds combined with melancholic melodic twists. Layering, field recordings, sampling and granular synthesis are just some of the sound design tools being used in creating syn- music.

Roygbiv

Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indaco, Violet, Black, White, Grey; is an electronic music project started in 2013. Sounds from all over the world are combined together to create smooth harmonies with tiny percussive elements in a form of dialog. Infinite reverbs and massive granular synthesis are key features of Roygbiv
