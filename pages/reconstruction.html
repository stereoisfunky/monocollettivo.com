(re)construction

Audiovisual Live Set.Padova Italy - 2014

Concept

"De-reconstruction" is a collage of live sets that investigates the theme of the destruction and reconstruction of the music of the twentieth Century. Four authors in four different countries have collaborated online with the interpretation of the theme. The result is one and only one.

Goal

Fill the space and time barriers that make difficult remote collaborations and at the same time emphasize the expressive and cultural differences as essential form.
